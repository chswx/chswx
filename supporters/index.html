About @chswx

@chswx is community-supported, hype-averse weather information, preparedness tips, and alerts for the Charleston, SC Tri-County area (Berkeley, Charleston, Dorchester counties) by Jared Smith.

Follow @chswx

Follow Charleston Weather updates on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram. Data for this page is provided by Weather Underground and is sourced from the official National Weather Service forecast.
